About Us

Started in 2015 as a Level 2 clinic, Furaha Medical Care has grown through commitment into a **Level 4 Licensed Hospital** accredited by the NHIF.

Our Mission

To provide compassionate, accessible, high quality, cost effective healthcare to the community.

Our Vision

To lead the evolution of healthcare to enable every member of the community to enjoy a better healthier life.

Core Values

  • Safety first and foremost
  • Honesty and confidentiality
  • Timely service delivery
  • Patient-centered care
