What we offer

Integrated services that keep care simple for patients and families

Our departments work closely together so every visit can move smoothly from assessment to testing, treatment, referral, and follow-up.

Outpatient and diagnostic care

Walk-in patients receive prompt assessment, treatment, and same-day support from our consultation rooms, laboratory, and pharmacy team.

  • General consultation for adults and children
  • Medical laboratory tests and diagnostics
  • Pharmacy with quality, affordable medication
  • Dental and optical services for routine and urgent needs

Admission, maternity, and surgery

We support safe recovery with structured inpatient care, maternity services, theatre procedures, and attentive clinical monitoring.

  • Male, female, pediatric, and private wards
  • Maternity, newborn, and antenatal support
  • Main and minor theatre procedures
  • Comfort-focused admission and discharge planning

Rehabilitation, support, and outreach

Healing goes beyond treatment, which is why our support services strengthen recovery, prevention, and access within the wider community.

  • Ambulance transfer and urgent patient support
  • Nutrition, physiotherapy, and counseling
  • Maternal and child health clinics with immunization
  • Free outreach camps every six months for vulnerable families
